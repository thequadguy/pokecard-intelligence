This One's For You Too

"You had a favourite card. Maybe it was a Charizard, maybe it was something nobody else cared about — but you cared. That feeling never really went away."

The Rare Pick started with a box of childhood cards found at the back of a closet. One look and it all came flooding back — the excitement of a new pack, the hunt for that one card, the pure joy of it. Sound familiar?

This is a place for anyone rediscovering that feeling. No experts here. No gatekeeping. Just someone genuinely learning, hunting, and sharing the ride — chasing cards with cool art, vintage holos that shimmer just right, and the ones that make you stop and think I need that.

If any of that sounds like you — welcome home.
